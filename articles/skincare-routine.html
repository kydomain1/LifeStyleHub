Skincare Science

Skincare shouldn't be complicated. Based on scientific principles and individual skin characteristics, build a simple yet effective skincare routine that brings out your skin's natural radiance. This guide will help you understand your skin's needs and create a personalized care regimen.

1

Understanding Your Skin Type

The first step to effective skincare is accurately determining your skin type. Through simple observation and touch tests, you can identify your skin's basic characteristics and lay the foundation for selecting appropriate products.

Skin Type Identification

  • Normal Skin: Balanced oil and water, soft to touch, rarely experiences problems
  • Dry Skin: Lacks oil, tends to feel tight, may appear flaky, needs deep moisturizing
  • Oily Skin: Excessive sebum production, enlarged pores, prone to breakouts
  • Combination Skin: Oily T-zone, dry cheeks, requires zone-specific care
  • Sensitive Skin: Easily irritated, prone to redness and allergic reactions, needs gentle products
Choose products specifically designed for your skin type
2

The Essential Three-Step Routine

Regardless of skin type, everyone needs to follow the three core steps of skincare: cleanse, moisturize, and protect. This forms the foundation of healthy skin and serves as the prerequisite for any advanced treatments.

Golden Rule of Skincare: Simple and consistent. Complex routines aren't necessarily more effective—the key is choosing suitable products and using them consistently.

Core Care Steps

  • Gentle Cleansing: Choose a cleanser suitable for your skin, cleanse morning and evening
  • Appropriate Moisturizing: All skin types need moisture—choose products with suitable texture and ingredients
  • Daily Protection: Use SPF 30+ sunscreen daily—the most important anti-aging measure
Consistency is the key to skincare success
3

Advanced Treatment Ingredients

Once your basic routine is stable, you can add functional ingredients based on specific needs. Understanding these ingredients' mechanisms and usage methods can help you better address skin concerns.

Ingredient Addition Principle: Introduce only one new ingredient at a time, observe skin reactions for 2-4 weeks before considering additional products.

Common Active Ingredients

  • Vitamin C: Antioxidant, brightens complexion, recommended for morning use
  • Retinol: Promotes cell renewal, reduces fine lines, evening use only
  • Hyaluronic Acid: Powerful hydration, suitable for all skin types
  • Niacinamide: Controls oil, minimizes pores, reduces inflammation
Start simple and gradually build a complete care routine
4

Secrets to Skincare Success

Skincare is a process requiring patience and consistency. Understand your skin's physiological cycle, allow sufficient time to observe results, and adjust your care regimen based on seasonal, age-related, and environmental factors.

Time Rule: Skincare products typically need 4-6 weeks to show noticeable effects. Give your skin and products adequate time.
