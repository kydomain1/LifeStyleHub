Handbag Buying Guide

What to Look For

  • Leather Quality: Full-grain leather is the highest quality and most durable
  • Construction: Look for reinforced stitching and quality hardware
  • Size: Consider your daily needs and lifestyle
  • Versatility: Choose colors and styles that complement your wardrobe
  • Functionality: Ensure adequate organization and storage

Care Tips

  • Store in dust bags when not in use
  • Use leather conditioner every 6 months
  • Avoid prolonged sun exposure
  • Clean spills immediately with a soft cloth
  • Stuff with tissue paper to maintain shape
