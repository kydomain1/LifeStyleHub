Frequently Asked Questions

How do you select products for review?

We carefully research and test products based on quality, value, user reviews, and alignment with our values of sustainability and authenticity. Our editorial team evaluates hundreds of products to bring you only the best recommendations.

Do you accept product submissions for review?

Yes, we welcome product submissions from brands and manufacturers. Please email us at hello@lifestylehub.com with detailed product information, and our editorial team will review your submission.

How can I contribute as a guest writer?

We occasionally accept guest contributions from industry experts and lifestyle professionals. Please send us your writing samples, expertise area, and proposed article topics for consideration.

Are your reviews sponsored or biased?

We maintain editorial independence in all our reviews. While we may receive products for testing, our opinions are never influenced by brands or advertisers. All partnerships and sponsorships are clearly disclosed.

Can I republish your content?

Our content is copyrighted. For republishing requests, please contact us with details about your intended use. We're happy to discuss licensing and partnership opportunities.

How often do you update your content?

We regularly update our articles and reviews to ensure accuracy and relevance. Product recommendations are reviewed quarterly, and trending topics are covered as they emerge.
